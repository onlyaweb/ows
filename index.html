  • O Pastor ->

    Herding in extended mode

    The shepherd aims to be a web space for sharing about extensive shepherding, and a portrait of this secular activity. It also functions as a living laboratory for the Green Data Earth project. It is made up of a herd of autochthonous breeds of Churras do Minho, Garranos and Cachenas.

  • Desalinhar ->

    Yarns & Textiles

    This Wool Enhancement programme brings together knowledge and different audiences, with varied interests, in order to amplify the multidisciplinary potential of this raw material and also serves to support tour operators, sheep producers and the general public. It is a programme that has a positive social impact, reactivating and highlighting the empirical knowledge of senior citizens and their role as actors in the semantics of intangible heritage, memory and identity.
    2023-Pause

  • Escola da Serra ->

    Learn to Teach

    Escola da Serra is a place where the past meets the future, uniting ancestral know-how with contemporary creation in a living territory. A school-laboratory in a rural environment where traditional knowledge (handicrafts, agriculture, community practices) meets contemporary practices (art, design, sustainability), fostering the cultural, social and environmental regeneration of the territory.
